Valemount

Website design for a construction company

The Brief

This company did not previously have a website and needed a place to showcase their skills and high-quality workmanship to help drive new sales and potential clients.

The Solution

As Valemount's main USP was their craftsmanship, this design focusses around the high quality of the build and materials through use of large images and textures to create interest, particularly focusing on woodwork and glass elements.
