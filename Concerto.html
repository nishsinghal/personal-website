Concerto

Homepage concept for a management consultancy

The Brief

This company's website looked dated, having been built using tables in the 90's. It needed a complete refresh. The client wanted something that kept their logo colours but which gave an impression of finesse and of a larger organisation than they currently were.

The Solution

I created a rebrand using the musical association of the firm's name to emphasise elements of its brand promise that are in common with an orchestra, such as collaboration, finesse and deep functional expertise. The glossy look and feel gives a sense of quality.
